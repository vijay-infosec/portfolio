About Me

Cybersecurity did not start for me as a casual interest. It became a discipline once I realised how much risk can hide behind systems that appear perfectly normal.

Threats do not announce themselves. They blend in, mimic legitimacy, and rely on inattention. This pushed me to focus on behavioural analysis and early indicators rather than surface-level signals.
